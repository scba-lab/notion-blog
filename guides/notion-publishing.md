## Step 4: Paste into Notion

1. Click in the page body area (below the properties)
2. Paste (Cmd+V)
3. Notion will automatically format:
   - ## headings → Notion H2 headings
   - ### headings → Notion H3 headings
   - Code blocks → Notion code blocks
   - Lists → Notion lists
   - Bold/italic → Formatted text

## Step 5: Save and Check

1. Save the page in Notion
2. Wait 60 seconds (for ISR to pick up the change)
3. Visit your blog: https://notion-blog-cosqyu0km-sunghun-juns-projects.vercel.app
4. Your post should appear on the homepage!
5. Click it to see the full post
